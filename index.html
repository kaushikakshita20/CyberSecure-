The 3 Pillars of Cybersecurity

🔒

Confidentiality

Only authorised people can see sensitive information. Enforced using passwords and encryption.

✅

Integrity

Data must stay accurate and not be tampered with. Achieved through hashing and digital signatures.

⚡

Availability

Systems must be accessible when needed. Protected via backups and DDoS mitigation.
